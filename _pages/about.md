I am a PhD student working on the **perception and evaluation of biases in applications of LLMs to biomedicine** at [Loria](https://www.loria.fr/en/), Université de Lorraine (France), since October 2024. I am supervised by [Karën Fort](https://members.loria.fr/KFort/) (Loria, Université de Lorraine, France) and [Aurélie Névéol](https://perso.limsi.fr/neveol/) (LISN, Université Paris-Saclay, France).

My research interests include:  
- ethical aspects of biomedical NLP
- characterization and measurement of bias in (medical) LLM
- sociotechnology, real-world implementation of NLP technologies and downstream impacts
- human perception of bias, collective representations about LLMs 
- "NLP for NLP", analysis of scientific discourse, overclaiming detection


You can contact me at **clementine.bleuze@univ-lorraine.fr**.
